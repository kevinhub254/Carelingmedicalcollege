Career

Welcome to the Career Services Department at Careling College. Our mission is to help students and alumni achieve their career goals by providing comprehensive career services, including:

  • Career counseling and assessments
  • Resume and cover letter reviews
  • Job search advice and resources
  • Internship and job placement assistance
  • Networking opportunities with employers and alumni

Our Career Services team is dedicated to helping you build a strong foundation for long-term success. Whether you are a current student or an alumnus, we are here to support you in your career journey.
